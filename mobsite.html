Let's say a mobile site

What is a mobile site?

A mobile site is a web page adapted for viewing on the screens of smartphones and tablets. Such sites have an intuitive interface, fast loading and easy navigation.

Why is this important?

Today, more than 70% of users access the Internet through mobile devices. Therefore, a mobile website is not just an option, but a necessity for any business.
